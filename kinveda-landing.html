How KinVeda Works

From your first assessment to ongoing care in four simple steps

1

Take the Assessment

A personalized 4-section intake form determines your needs and connects you to the right specialist.

2

Get Matched

Admin reviews your request and sends a curated invite to book with a matched KinMentor.

3

Pay & Connect

Secure payment unlocks your video session. A calendar invite is sent to both you and your counselor.

4

Ongoing Care

After your first session, subscribe to a monthly package with bi-weekly calls. Cancel anytime.
