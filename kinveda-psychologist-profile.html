👋 About Dr. Ananya Mehta

"I believe that the family is not just a context — it is the client. My work helps individuals untangle themselves from unhealthy family patterns, while building new, healthier bonds."

I'm a Clinical Psychologist with 9 years of experience working specifically with nuclear and only-child family systems in urban India. I completed my M.Phil in Clinical Psychology from NIMHANS, Bangalore, and hold RCI registration (License #A-23814).

My practice is rooted in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), with a strong understanding of India's unique generational pressure dynamics — particularly the "only child trap" where 100% of parental hopes and anxieties land on a single child. I work in Hindi and English, and I am deeply familiar with the pressures of urban Indian nuclear families navigating career, marriage, and cultural conflict.

🧠 My Therapeutic Approach

I use a structured yet warm, evidence-based approach. Every session is goal-oriented, but also leaves space for you to feel truly heard. My style:

🔍

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identifying and challenging the core beliefs that drive conflict and anxiety in your family relationships.

🌿

Validation Over Advice

I spend the first 15 minutes of every session listening — without offering solutions — so you feel genuinely heard.

🧬

Family Systems Mapping

Using genograms to map multi-generational patterns so we can break cycles — not just manage symptoms.

📋

Practical Homework

Each session comes with structured exercises — boundary scripts, journaling prompts, and "third space" building activities.
